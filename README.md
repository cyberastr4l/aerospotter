# AeroSpotter: Talent Show Edition

**AeroSpotter** is a bold, high-contrast aircraft and airport identification game designed in a clean, modern "Runway Brutalist" style. It was custom-built for a school Talent Show to test spotting skills in a fast-paced, interactive format.

## How to Play

1. **Start the Quiz**: Click **Launch Game** (or **Iniciar Jogo**) to begin. The deck contains a randomized selection of aircraft silhouettes and airport locations.
2. **Reveal the Card**: Click the locked card to reveal the airplane silhouette or the airport code. A **10-second shutter countdown** begins immediately!
3. **Identify**: Call out the name of the aircraft or the airport out loud before the shutter closes.
4. **Reveal & Validate**: Click **Reveal Answer** to show the correct name. The host then clicks **Correct** or **Incorrect** to immediately update the progress and advance to the next round.
5. **Debriefing**: At the end of the run, view the final flight archive report summarizing your performance.

## Key Features

*   **Airport Overlay**: Airport rounds feature a unique overlay displaying the official 3-letter airport code directly over the airfield image.
*   **10-Second Shutter Timer**: Simulates rapid spotting conditions with a visual timer bar.
*   **Dark Mode**: High-contrast dark theme toggle positioned at the top-right of the screen.
*   **Dual Language**: Smooth translation support switching between English and European Portuguese (PT-PT).
*   **Minimalist Interface**: Free of player name entry forms, complex HUD stats, or distracting scores—tailored for presentation on a big screen during live events.
